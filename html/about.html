Who We Are?

Feeling overwhelmed with configuring your smart home?

I offer personalized coaching services to help individuals like you unlock the full potential of your smart home. Whether you’re a beginner or have some experience with the technology, I can provide you with step-by-step guidance to assist you with setting up your system. This includes planning your setup and configuration, selecting smart home devices, and creating personalized home automations. With a personalized approach, you will be on your way to creating the smart home of your dream.

I will also be able to provide you with troubleshooting tips and tricks to quickly and easily resolve any issues you may encounter while interacting with your system. I have extensive experience setting up and optimizing smart home automation platforms such as Home Assistant, SmartThings, and Apple HomeKit for convenience, comfort, security, accessibility, and energy savings.

If you’re interested in receiving personalized coaching to configure your smart home, please fill out the form below to request a free 30-minute initial consultation. I look forward to helping you unlock the full potential of your smart home, one motion at a time!
